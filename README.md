# 236 TechnoTicks Robot 2024
The code for our 2024 robot, "Soundwave," which was 25-19-1 in official play in 2024 and ranked 31st in the NE district (9th in CT), having earned 151 points. 
Our robot was also notable for being the best in the world for scoring traps, with an expected Endgame EPA of 9.2 !!!
